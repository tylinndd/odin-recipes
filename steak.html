Peppered Steak

Description

This slow cooker pepper steak always comes out tender and flavorful and is one of our family's favorites. It's great to make ahead of time and serve over rice, egg noodles, or chow mein.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef sirloin, cut into 2 inch strips
  • ¾ teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste
  • tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • ¼ cup hot water
    • tablespoon cornstarch
  • ½ cup chopped onion

Steps

  1. Season beef with garlic powder and pepper. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef and cook until browned, about 5 minutes.
  2. Transfer beef to a slow cooker. Dissolve bouillon cube in hot water; pour over beef. Stir in cornstarch and onion.
  3. Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours or on High for 3 to 4 hours.
